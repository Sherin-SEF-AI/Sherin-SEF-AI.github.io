AMBIENT SAFETY INTELLIGENCE: A Framework for Proactive Public Safety Through Distributed Multi-Source Data Correlation

This paper introduces the Safety Ecosystem Framework, a novel architectural approach addressing fundamental limitations in emergency response and crime prevention systems. While food can be delivered in ten minutes, emergency help often takes thirty minutes or more. This research presents a comprehensive framework for ambient safety intelligence that unifies heterogeneous data sources into a cohesive ecosystem, reducing emergency response times from 25-30 minutes down to under 5 minutes through automated detection and optimized coordination.
